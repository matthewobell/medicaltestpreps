Privacy Policy

Last updated: October 29, 2025

Medical Test Prep, LLC (“we,” “us,” or “our”) operates the EMIGS Prep mobile application (the “App”). This Privacy Policy explains what information we collect, how it is used, and your choices.

Information We Collect

Learning and usage data:
The App collects information about how you use learning features, such as quiz attempts, selected answers, scores, progress, flashcard interactions, study behavior, and feature usage.

This information is primarily stored on your device. In limited cases, certain usage or diagnostic data may be transmitted in a privacy-preserving way to help operate, maintain, and improve the App.

Account information (optional):
Some features may allow or require account creation, such as email sign-in or premium access. If you create an account, we may collect basic account information such as your email address and profile details you choose to provide.

Support communications:
If you choose to contact us, you may provide an email address and message content. This information is used solely to respond to your request.

Diagnostics and stability data:
We may receive anonymized diagnostic or crash information (for example, through device or operating system settings) to help improve performance and reliability. This data is governed by your device platform’s privacy controls.

How We Use Information

  • Provide core educational functionality (quizzes, flashcards, progress tracking).
  • Improve learning quality, content effectiveness, and app performance.
  • Maintain app stability and security.
  • Respond to support requests.
  • Operate optional account and premium features.

Data Linked to You

Learning activity may be associated with your device or account if you choose to sign in. We do not collect personal information such as contacts, photos, messages, or activity outside the App.

Support emails are used only for communication related to your request.

Analytics & Tracking

We do not use third-party advertising SDKs or cross-app tracking technologies. Any analytics collected are used solely to understand app usage and improve the product.

Children’s Privacy

The App is intended for medical trainees and professionals and is not directed toward children under 13 (or the minimum age required by applicable law).

Data Security

We take reasonable measures to protect information stored or transmitted through the App. No system is completely secure, and we cannot guarantee absolute security.

Your Choices

  • Reset or delete local study data using in-app options.
  • Uninstall the App to remove locally stored data.
  • Choose whether to create an account or contact support.

Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Changes will be posted here with an updated “Last updated” date.
