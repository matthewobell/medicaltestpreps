About Us

Medical Test Prep was developed by an Associate Professor and Minimally Invasive Surgeon to help residents in all surgical subspecialties pass their Fundamental of Laparoscopic Surgery (FLS) written component and Essentials in Minimally Invasive Gynecologic Surgery (EMIGS) cognitive examination.

Medical Test Prep is not affiliated with the FLS program, SAGES, AAGL, the American College of Surgeons (ACS) or the EMIGS program.

We are constantly looking to improve this app. Feedback can be sent via Send Feedback in Settings. Best of luck as you continue your journey in medicine, we're here to help!

- The Medical Test Prep Team
