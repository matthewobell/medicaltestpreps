Medical Test Prep is the best way to prep for EMIGS!

Medical Test Prep, LLC is a physician-led medical education company committed to empowering residents and attending physicians through high-yield, app-based learning tools. Founded by a board-certified physician, a skilled tech team, and part-time legal support, our mission is to promote lifelong learning and board success across a wide range of specialties.

We create short, focused question bank exams that fit seamlessly into the rare gaps in a busy physician's schedule. Our approach is grounded in educational research: brief, frequent bursts of learning outperform cramming and foster deeper, longer-lasting knowledge retention. These tools are designed not only to help physicians succeed on high-stakes exams, but also to strengthen clinical excellence throughout their careers.
